10 FORENSIC SIGNALS

Each signal targets a specific failure mode unique to AI image generators like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion.

SIGNAL 01
🔬
PIXEL & SENSOR NOISE
Real cameras produce photon shot noise. AI images are unnaturally clean — near-zero noise floor is a primary synthetic signature.
SIGNAL 02
🌊
FFT SPECTRAL PATTERN
GAN upsampling leaves characteristic 2px checkerboard artifacts in the frequency domain. Stable Diffusion shows unique spectral periodicity.
SIGNAL 03
📊
ELA COMPRESSION
Error Level Analysis exposes freshly generated images — no editing history means abnormally uniform block compression variance.
SIGNAL 04
👁️
SKIN & FACE TEXTURE
AI skin is too smooth — no pores, no micro-texture variation. Luminance std-dev below threshold flags plasticky AI faces.
SIGNAL 05
🎨
COLOR HISTOGRAM
AI generators produce suspiciously smooth, bell-curved histograms and abnormally low saturation variance — real photos are messier.
SIGNAL 06
✋
HAND ANATOMY
AI consistently fails at hands — extra fingers, merged digits, impossible joints. We count protrusions and measure boundary complexity.
SIGNAL 07
💡
LIGHTING PHYSICS
Shadows in conflicting directions, misplaced specular highlights — AI treats lighting as decoration, not physics.
SIGNAL 08
🌆
BACKGROUND COHERENCE
AI deprioritizes backgrounds — warped objects, repeating texture tiles, and impossible depth are hallmarks of generation artifacts.
SIGNAL 09
📐
TEXT & GEOMETRY
Curved straight lines, nonsense text, broken vanishing points — AI fails spatial reasoning that real cameras record perfectly.
SIGNAL 10
⚙️
ENSEMBLE AGGREGATION
Weighted combination with anatomy and physics boosts. Multiple high signals trigger cascading confidence multipliers.
